All About AI




AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is a cutting-edge field of computer science focused on creating intelligent machines. It aims to replicate human-like cognitive abilities such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI can be narrow, specialized in specific tasks, or general, with broader capabilities akin to human intelligence. Techniques like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision drive AI's advancements. As AI continues to progress, it holds the potential to revolutionize various industries and transform the way we interact with technology.
